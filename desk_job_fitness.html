Desk Stretches for Lower Back Pain

Working long hours at a desk can wreak havoc on your lower back. Try this simple stretch routine twice a day to ease tension and strengthen your muscles:

  • Seated Spinal Twist
  • Standing Hamstring Stretch
  • Child’s Pose Desk Version

How to Stay Active While Working from Home

Break the sedentary cycle! Here are three easy ways to stay active without leaving your home office:

  • Set a timer for hourly stand-up breaks.
  • Use an under-desk elliptical or resistance bands during meetings.
  • Commit to a 10-minute movement session before or after work.

Simple Desk Yoga Poses for Stress Relief

Desk yoga is a fantastic way to reduce stress and refocus your mind during the workday:

  • Seated Cat-Cow Stretch
  • Seated Forward Bend
  • Neck Rolls and Shoulder Shrugs

Best Wrist Stretches for Computer Users

Combat wrist strain with these quick and effective stretches:

  • Wrist Flexor Stretch
  • Wrist Extensor Stretch
  • Prayer Stretch
